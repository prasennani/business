Thanks For The Results Achieved With You

Thank you for considering a donation to support the initiatives and projects undertaken by the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Krishna Alumni Association (JNVKAA). Your contribution will make a significant diDerence in the lives of students and the community we serve.

1. Fill out the Donation Form:

Please fill out the form beside with your name, batch number, email address, and upload a screenshot or any document of the payment confirmation for our records. Your batch number helps us track the contributions and maintain transparency.

2. Select Mode of Payment:

Choose your preferred mode of payment from the options provided below. We accept donations via various methods, including bank transfers, UPI payments, cash payments, and more. Select the option that is most convenient for you.

3. Specify Purpose of Payment:

In the "Purpose of Payment" section, please specify the purpose of your donation and the amount you wish to contribute. Whether it's for supporting educational initiatives, infrastructure development, scholarships, or any other specific project, your donation will be directed towards the designated purpose.

4. Submit the Form:

After filling out the form and specifying the details, proceed to submit the form. Instructions for each payment method will be provided upon submission of the form
