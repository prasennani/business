Welcome to the official JNV Krishna Alumni Website, where the past meets the present and future!

JNVKAA (Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Krishna Alumni Association) is a vibrant community of alumni from JNV Krishna dedicated to fostering connections, supporting our alma mater, and empowering the next generation of leaders. Join us to stay connected, give back, and make a difference together.
